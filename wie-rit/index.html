ELECTRONIC SCRAMBLE

EEE & EIE

080 23317531

LHC Seminar-Hall-1

9th MARCH

RULES

  • Round 1: Minute to win it: This is a rapid fire round and the teams that answer most number of questions in 1 minute advance to round 2.
  • Round 2 : 1 ohm- A set of resistors with different values will be provided. Use them in parallel/series and make an equivalent resistance equal to 1 ohm.
  • Round 3 : Innovation Test: A real life problem is given to the teams in this final round and the teams must come up with innovative ideas to find a solution to it. The team with the best idea wins!

    • TECHNOFRENZY

    ECE & TC &ML

    080 23317531

    LHC Seminar-Hall-1

    8th MARCH

    RULES

  • Round 1 : One person in a team will be given a component and a list of words related to the component will be given.This person should make the team mates guess the component without using any of the words that are a part of the list.
  • Round 2 : Each team will be given a basic gates circuits with either the input or output already specified. Use logical operations to find the respective answer.
  • Round 3 : Each person in the team will be given a task and these tasks must be performed one after the other. The Team finishing these tasks first wins.
